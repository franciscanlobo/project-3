About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli, founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, is a celebrated Japanese animation studio known for its heartfelt stories and stunning hand-drawn worlds. With films like My Neighbor Totoro, Spirited Away, and Princess Mononoke, the studio has become beloved worldwide for its imaginative settings, memorable characters, and emotional depth—cementing its legacy as a cornerstone of animated cinema.
